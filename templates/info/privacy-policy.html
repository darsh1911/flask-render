Privacy Policy

Bharat Mechanic, a brand under Five Tyres Automotive Services Private Limited ("Bharat Mechanic," "we," "us," or "our"), is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website (the "Site") and our mobile application (the "App"), collectively referred to as the "Platform."

1.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you access or use the Platform

This information includes, but is not limited to

  • Contact information (such as name, email address, phone number)
  • Location information
  • Vehicle information (such as make, model, license plate number)
  • Payment information (such as credit card details)
  • Communications and interactions with us (such as service requests, feedback, and support inquiries)

2.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes.

This includes, but is not limited to

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Processing payments and transactions
  • Communicating with you about our services, promotions, and updates
  • Analyzing usage trends and preferences to enhance user experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

3.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

In addition to the purposes outlined in Section 2, we may share your information, including payment information, with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • With third-party payment processors and financial institutions to process payments and transactions made on the Platform. Please note that we do not store or have access to your complete payment card details, as they are securely processed and stored by the payment processor in compliance with industry standards and regulations.
  • With our authorized service providers and business partners who assist us in facilitating payments, fraud prevention, and other financial activities related to our services.

4.

Data Security

Rest assured. Your data is in safe hands.

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, including payment information, from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that while we strive to maintain the security of your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5.

Your Choices and Rights

You may choose not to provide certain personal information, including payment information, but this may limit your ability to access certain features or services of the Platform. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, subject to applicable legal requirements.

6.

Children's Privacy

The Platform is not directed to individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on the Platform or by other means.
